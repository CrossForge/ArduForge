Detailed Description

Implements communication with an HC-SR04 ultrasonic distance measurement device.

The HC-SR04 produces an ultrasonic sound and measures the time it takes for the signal to travel back to the sensor. According to the data sheet range is from 2 to 400 cm with an accuracy or 3 mm (specifications may differ between vendors). In any case the distance measurement highly depends on the targeted surface. Plain solid surfaces work best, while materials such as clothing can absorb all of the ultrasonic impulse which makes measurement impossible.
