\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements communication with an H\+C-\/\+S\+R04 ultrasonic distance measurement device. 

The H\+C-\/\+S\+R04 produces an ultrasonic sound and measures the time it takes for the signal to travel back to the sensor. According to the data sheet range is from 2 to 400 cm with an accuracy or $\pm $ 3 mm (specifications may differ between vendors). In any case the distance measurement highly depends on the targeted surface. Plain solid surfaces work best, while materials such as clothing can absorb all of the ultrasonic impulse which makes measurement impossible.
